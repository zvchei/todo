# A Simple yet Flexible Task Organizer

## Requirements

* Multi-platform support (Linux, Android, Web)
* Task structure:
    * Standard fields: title, description, due date, priority, status
    * Output: tasks have results - inventory (items or data)
    * Dependencies: tasks can depend on other tasks and on inventory
    * Tasks can be grouped into composite tasks (e.g. "buy groceries") that can be fully or partially completed and provide inventory for other tasks
    * Taks can be grouped in projects semantically for better organization
    * Automatically place tasks in composites based on their content and dependencies

## Future extensions

* Task templates
* Recurring tasks
* Task scheduling and reminders including geolocation-based reminders
* Task sharing and collaboration
* Handoff of tasks between users and AI agents
* Integration with calendar and other applications
